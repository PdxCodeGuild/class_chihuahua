Red Panda Short Bio

The Red Panda is a lesser panda native to eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. It is closely related to racoons. It is not related to the giant panda and it is not a bear. The Red Panda has long wrist bones that act as false thumbs. Extinct species of the Red Panda date back to 25 million years ago. You can find Red Pandas climbing trees. The species is currently in the endangered list since 2015. Red Pandas primarly feast on bamboo. It is the state animal of Sikkim.
